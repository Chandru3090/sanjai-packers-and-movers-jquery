Our Process

Sanjai Packers & Movers are provide fast, cost-effective moving & providing services.

Consultation and Assessment

The process starts with a consultation to understand the client's moving needs, followed by an on-site assessment to determine the scope and requirements of the move.

Quotation and Scheduling

Based on the assessment, a detailed quotation is provided, and a mutually convenient moving date and time are scheduled to begin the relocation process.

Packing and Labeling

A professional packing team uses appropriate materials to securely pack items, ensuring their safety during transportation. Each item is labeled for easy identification.

Loading and Transportation

Packed belongings are loaded onto specially equipped vehicles and transported to the designated destination, adhering to safety regulations and timelines.

Unloading and Setup (Optional)

Upon arrival, the team unloads items and can assist with unpacking and setting up if requested by the client, ensuring a smooth transition.

Quality Check and Payment

A final quality check is performed to verify that all items are in good condition, and the client settles the invoice for the services provided, completing the move process.
